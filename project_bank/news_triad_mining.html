In this project, I was given a dataset containing news categories and titles and tasked with finding the most frequent term triads. I utilised my knowledge of the Spark programming language to design both an RDD (Resilient Distributed Dataset) and regular dataframe implementation.

Through this project, I learned how to filter datasets given a list of stopwords, perform relative frequency computations to compare the occurences of different triad combinations, and how to utilise broadcast variables and UDFs (User Defined Functions) to streamline the program pipeline.
